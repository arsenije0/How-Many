ABOUT HOW MANY

There are 7 billion humans on this planet doing stuff that humans do, much like you. Divided by invisible lines we call countries, humans are for the most part segregated from the vastness of humanity. Ever wondered how many of your countrymen there are in comparison to some other congregation of people? How do you fair economically as a collective? Well, in that case, this is the right place to acquire that perspective.
